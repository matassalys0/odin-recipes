Pancakes

Description

This is a paleo version of traditional pancakes. Serve with butter.

Ingredients

  • 1 banana, mashed
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon arrowroot powder

Steps

  1. Blend banana, egg, and arrowroot powder in a blender until well mixed.
  2. Heat a griddle or large skillet over medium heat. Divide batter into two portions; ladle onto griddle. Cook until bubbles form, 2 to 3 minutes; flip and brown on the other side, 2 to 3 minutes more.
